Continuing Personal / Professional Development.

CPD or Continuing Personal / Professional Development is about furthering yourself through extracurricular activities and participating in events outside of University time. You use this time to help grow as a person, strengthening your skills and bolstering your weaknesses. You build confidence and learn to better handle yourself in a work environment. These events take place in tandem with academic studies for university. Events can consist of volunteering at your local homeless shelter, visiting student run social societies such as DevSoc and NTU Systems Society or even sporting events such as football or basketball.
