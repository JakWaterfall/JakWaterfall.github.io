Work.

I was contracted out for my Excel expertise to Jane McKay at RDA. My goal was to build excel spreadsheets in order to be populated by their staff and then presented to their senior leaders. The information gathered was on who attended their group sessions during the national lockdown and the kind of care they required.

I worked with one of their employees who knew the specification (and the type of data) the spreadsheet had to accommodate and I built the sheet including all of the formulas, data validation, pivot tables, conditional formatting etc. When things would break after letting their staff use them or we missed data / new important data came up that needed to be added, I worked with the same employee to maintain the sheets until everybody was happy working with them and the senior staff was happy with the results and information acquired from them.
