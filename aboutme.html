About me.

Hi! I’m Jak Waterfall and I’m a first-year computer science student currently studying at Nottingham Trent university. I have ambitions to one day be a software developer but for right now I must learn my trade. In my free time I like to program little games in java and python and make basic android apps for fun. I also enjoy playing video games, drinking with my friends and spending quality time with my family.


Before university I was a fully qualified electrician, I mostly worked on commercial properties such as TK Maxx and Currys. I found myself growing tired of this work and displeased with the career path I had chosen for myself. I decided I needed a change in my life and to pursue a career in computer science.
